# CPIMS Governance Charter
## Commissioner Public Interaction Management System

This document governs the execution model for CPIMS under the Antigravity multi-agent delivery approach.

---

## 1. Purpose

CPIMS is executed as a **multi-agent, artifact-driven, demo-first program**.

Antigravity is used as a **control plane for disciplined parallel delivery**, not as an unconstrained coding assistant.

Every new capability must pass through:

1. truth definition
2. controlled lane execution
3. browser/runtime verification

before it is considered complete.

---

## 2. Mission

Build a polished, reliable, citizen-centric digital service platform for Commissioner office public interaction management.

CPIMS is not a generic appointment application. It is a **public interaction operating system prototype** that must demonstrate:

- clear public access rules
- guided citizen intake
- preservation of walk-in access where applicable
- disciplined staff triage and scheduling
- transparent citizen tracking
- executive visibility
- credible AI-ready capability surfaces

The immediate target is a **high-clarity, high-discipline, commissioner-impressing working prototype**.

---

## 3. Core Success Criterion

The prototype is successful only if it convincingly demonstrates a:

**world-class, citizen-centric, operationally disciplined public interaction platform**

where:

- citizens clearly understand access rules
- staff operate within controlled workflow discipline
- leadership sees live operational value
- all major surfaces reflect the same underlying truth

---

## 4. Global Non-Negotiables

### 4.1 Business Truth is Central
No lane may redefine:
- day-wise access logic
- lifecycle transitions
- citizen/public information visibility rules
- metric meanings

All such truth is governed through `TRUTH_REGISTRY.md`.

### 4.2 Advisory AI Only
AI outputs are advisory unless explicitly approved otherwise.

### 4.3 No Fake Metrics
All metrics must derive from:
- shared store truth
- seeded scenario truth
- modeled event truth

### 4.4 Verification is Mandatory
No phase is complete until browser/runtime verification is performed and artifacts are reviewed.

### 4.5 Demo Clarity Over Technical Novelty
Execution must optimize for:
- clarity
- trust
- workflow discipline
- operational credibility
- demo reliability

---

## 5. Execution Lanes

## Lane A — Product Contract Guardian
**Mandate:** Protect truth.

**Owns**
- business rules
- lifecycle truth
- public/internal information boundaries
- schedule definitions
- metric definitions
- AI labeling truth
- notification truth
- truth registry updates

**Principle**
This lane is the source of truth. No feature lane may violate it.

---

## Lane B — Citizen Experience Builder
**Mandate:** Make the citizen experience unmistakably clear.

**Owns**
- homepage
- today’s rule visibility
- guidance flows
- submission flows
- acknowledgement
- tracking
- walk-in path
- citizen-facing copy clarity

**Success condition**
A first-time citizen immediately understands what applies today and what to do next.

---

## Lane C — Staff Operations Builder
**Mandate:** Make office handling look disciplined and administratively credible.

**Owns**
- request triage
- lifecycle actions
- slot assignment
- public office notes
- internal staff notes
- structured status enforcement
- staff decision surfaces
- communication intent controls
- AI staff-assist surfaces

**Success condition**
Office workflow feels controlled, useful, and decision-ready.

---

## Lane D — Executive Visibility Builder
**Mandate:** Make leadership feel immediate control and visibility.

**Owns**
- dashboard
- KPI summaries
- current day interaction mode
- category and status breakdowns
- recent requests
- communication KPIs
- AI-assist metrics
- intelligence surfaces in later phases

**Success condition**
Leadership sees immediate operational value within seconds.

---

## Lane E — Verification & Artifact Checker
**Mandate:** Validate reality through browser/runtime proof.

**Owns**
- golden path execution
- browser walkthrough validation
- screenshot capture
- mismatch reporting
- wording validation
- metric truth checks
- phase exit proof

**Success condition**
The integrated product works without ambiguity, inconsistency, or dead ends.

---

## 6. Three-Pass Methodology

Every new feature or phase must follow this sequence.

### Pass 1 — Foundation Truth
Define and lock:
- business truth contract
- lifecycle implications
- metric implications
- notification implications
- AI surface implications where applicable
- schema/store impacts
- seeded scenario impacts

**Outputs**
- feature contract update
- truth registry update
- schema/state change summary

### Pass 2 — Parallel Lane Build
Execute in parallel:
- Lane B — Citizen Experience
- Lane C — Staff Operations
- Lane D — Executive Visibility

**Rule**
Build lanes may consume truth, but may not redefine it.

**Outputs**
- working feature surfaces
- screenshots
- lane-level acceptance proof

### Pass 3 — Verification & Polish
Lane E validates:
- browser/runtime behavior
- wording clarity
- metric consistency
- citizen/staff/dashboard alignment
- demo readiness

**Outputs**
- mismatch report
- screenshot pack
- walkthrough artifact
- phase exit recommendation

---

## 7. Required Artifact Set

Every phase must produce:

- feature contract update
- schema/state change summary
- screenshot pack
- golden walkthrough note
- mismatch report
- unresolved risks note
- demo script delta

No phase may close without artifact review.

---

## 8. Governance Questions Before Every Phase

Before any phase begins, Lane A must answer:

1. What new truth is being introduced?
2. What existing truth is affected?
3. What must remain unchanged for citizen clarity?
4. What is modeled versus actually integrated?
5. What is public-facing versus staff-only versus dashboard-only?
6. What proof will show the phase is complete?

---

## 9. Final Operating Rule

Be autonomous **inside the contract**, not outside it.

If business truth is ambiguous:
- do not invent logic
- create a clarification artifact
- mark the work blocked until resolved
